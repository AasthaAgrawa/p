Musik

Musik is a minimalist music application that allows users to quickly play music from their library or playlists. Users can search for songs, like songs, and add them to playlists. Musik uses simple icons and gestures to keep the interface clear and visually appealing.

Project Information: Accenture Job Stimulation

Time: 4-5 hours · May 2025

Tools: Figma

Role: Product Designer

The Problem
Ability to see lyrics while listening to the song
Target Audience
iPhone users aged 16 to 21

My Process

  • 1. Understanding the Problem: Identified the core challenge and analyzed user research insights that emphasized a strong desire to view lyrics while listening.
  • 2. Defining the User: Defined the Gen Z listener as tech-savvy and multitasking; prioritized glanceability and visual appeal.
  • 3. Ideation & Exploration: Brainstormed lyric integration options like karaoke-style sync, scrollable text, and timestamp highlights.
  • 4. Wireframing & Prototyping: Built mid-fidelity wireframes in Figma and introduced a tap-to-flip feature for minimal disruption.
  • 5. User Testing & Iteration: Conducted peer feedback loops; adjusted font size, contrast, and transitions for readability.
  • 6. Final Design: Smooth lyrics view integrated seamlessly into UI, designed to increase emotional engagement and retention.
